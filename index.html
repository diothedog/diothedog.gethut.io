Pepperoni Pizza

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
pizza crust 1
marinara sauce 1 cup
shredded mozarella cheese 1 cup
pepperoni 20 slices

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

Step 2: Apply marinara sauce to crust, spreading evenly.

Step 3: Cover sauce with cheese.

Step 4: Add pepperoni on top of cheese.

Step 5: Bake for 20 minutes.

Step 6: Enjoy!

Classic Shrimp Scampi

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
kosher salt
linguini 12 oz
large shrimp, peeled and deveined 1 and 1/4 lb
extra virgin olive oil 1/3 cup
minced garlic 5 cloves
red pepper flakes 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon
dry white wine 1/3 cup
lemon juice and wedges from half a lemon
unsalted butter, cut into pieces 4 tablespoons
fresh parsley, finely chopped 1/4 cup

Step 1: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the linguine and cook as the label directs. Reserve 1 cup cooking water, then drain.

Step 2: Meanwhile, season the shrimp with salt. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook until the garlic is just golden, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Add the shrimp and cook, stirring occasionally, until pink and just cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side. Remove the shrimp to a plate. Add the wine and lemon juice to the skillet and simmer until slightly reduced, 2 minutes.

Step 3: Return the shrimp and any juices from the plate to the skillet along with the linguine, butter and 1/2 cup of the reserved cooking water. Continue to cook, tossing, until the butter is melted and the shrimp is hot, about 2 minutes, adding more of the reserved cooking water as needed. Season with salt; stir in the parsley. Serve with lemon wedges.

Chicken Stir-Fry

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
dark sesame oil, divided 2 tablespoons
garlic cloves, finely minced 2
chicken breasts, skinless and boneless 2 lbs
broccoli 1 head, stem removed
sliced mushrooms 1 dozen
carrots, peeled and julienned/td> 3
dry white wine 1/3 cup
diced green beans 1/4 lb
bok choy, chopped 1 head
teriyaki sauce 2 to 3 tablespoons

Step 1: Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a saute pan over medium heat. Add garlic and stir. Place the chicken in the pan and brown 4 minutes on each side. Remove from pan, slice into strips, set aside.

Step 2: Heat remaining tablespoon of oil in a wok over high heat. Add the vegetables and teriyaki sauce. Stir-fry quickly until the vegetables begin to soften. Add the chicken strips, combine well and continue to cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Serve immediately.

Source: Food Network
